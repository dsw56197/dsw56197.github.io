2017 Spring Break Snowboarding Trip

For my senior spring break, five of my friends and I took a ski trip to Colorado.

My friends and I boarded a flight to Colorado from Hartsfield-Jackson. After the three and a half hour flight, we made our way to Copper Mountain and got settled in. We rented skis and bought our slope passes for the week. We started out on the "baby" slopes, but by the end of the week we were getting onto the more dangerous and fun slopes. There was plenty of great, authentic food in the area as well; we went to many restaurants and had an amazing time. All in all, we had an outstanding time and I would love to do it again soon. It was by far the best trip I've ever been on.
